Inside Attendly

Everything HR needs, nothing it doesn't

A complete attendance & people platform — for owners, HR, managers and every employee.

Geo check-in / out

One-tap attendance with location and live status. Late, present and on-leave roll up to the dashboard in real time.

Leave & approvals

Employees apply, managers approve, balances update automatically. Cancel pending requests in a tap.

Payroll & comp-off

Salary payable computed from basic minus LOP. Comp-off is auto-derived from weekend & holiday attendance.

Reimbursements

Submit expense claims with receipts, route via manager to HR, bulk-approve, mark paid and export a ZIP of receipts.

Announcements

Post org-wide notices. Employees get a bell notification and you see read receipts across the team.

Excel reports & export

A full, editable HR database with styled multi-sheet .xlsx export — attendance, leave, payroll, all of it.

Native mobile app

Employees get a polished Android app — check in, view balances, apply for leave and file claims from their phone.

Secure & isolated

Every record is scoped to your organization with row-level security. Owners, HR, managers and employees each see exactly what they should.

Guided onboarding

An 8-step setup wizard gets your company, work week, holidays and team configured in minutes.

FAQ

Questions, answered

How is Attendly different from a spreadsheet?
It's a real multi-tenant platform: employees check in from their phone, leave and reimbursements flow through approvals automatically, payroll is computed for you, and everything exports to Excel when you need it. No formulas to maintain, no version mix-ups.
Is it really free for small teams?
Yes. Up to 5 employees is free for your whole 1-year period and renews free — with all core features and the mobile app. You only pay once you grow past 5 seats.
Where does the actual work happen?
This site (cataskkit.in) is our home. The product itself — sign-in, your admin console, onboarding and the employee app — lives at attendly.cataskkit.in. Every "Create a workspace" and "Sign in" button takes you straight there.
Can employees use it on their phone?
Absolutely — the employee experience is a polished, mobile-first native Android app. Owners, HR and managers use the desktop/web admin console for setup, approvals, payroll and reports.
How secure is our data?
Every record is scoped to your organization with database row-level security, so one company can never see another's data. Roles (owner, HR, manager, employee) control exactly what each person can access.
