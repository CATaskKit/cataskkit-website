4. Plans, fees & billing

Pricing is in Indian Rupees (INR) and is described on our Pricing page. In summary:

  • Every Workspace receives a 1-year period from the date it onboards.
  • Up to 5 employees is free for that whole period and renews free.
  • Above 5 employees, fees are charged per employee, per month, billed annually in advance at the applicable tier (₹25 for 6–10, ₹20 for 11–50, ₹18 for 51–100, ₹15 for 101+).
  • The optional Reimbursements add-on is ₹5 per employee per month (billed annually; free for Workspaces of 5 or fewer).
  • Raising your seat cap mid-period charges only the additional, prorated cost for the days remaining in your period; your period end date does not change. A fresh year is charged only at renewal.

Payments are processed by our payment gateway, Razorpay Software Private Limited ("Razorpay"). The price is computed and verified by our servers from the published tier table; we do not store your full card details. All fees are exclusive of applicable taxes unless stated otherwise. Subscriptions are for a fixed term and do not auto-renew or auto-charge without an action initiated by an Owner/HR admin.
