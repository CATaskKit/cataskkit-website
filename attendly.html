Capabilities

One platform, the whole HR workflow

From the moment someone joins to the day payroll runs — Attendly handles it.

Geo check-in / out

One-tap attendance with location capture and live hours. The check-in map shows exactly where each person clocked in.

Leave management

Multiple leave types, balances, applications and a clean approval queue for managers and HR. Cancel pending requests anytime.

Payroll & comp-off

Salary payable is basic minus loss-of-pay deductions. Comp-off is auto-derived from weekend and holiday attendance.

Reimbursements

Claims with receipt uploads, optional manager routing, bulk approve & mark paid, and a downloadable ZIP of receipts + report.

Announcements

Broadcast org-wide notices with bell notifications and read receipts so you know who's seen what.

Reports & Excel export

A full editable HR database with styled, multi-sheet .xlsx exports for attendance, leave and payroll.

Native mobile app

A mobile-first Android app for employees: check in, view balances, apply for leave and file claims on the go.

8-step onboarding

A guided wizard configures your company, work week, holidays and team — progress is saved as you go.

Roles & security

Owner, HR, manager and employee roles, with row-level security keeping each organization's data fully isolated.

Built for every role

One product, four points of view

Everyone sees exactly what they need — no more, no less.

Owner

Set up the company, manage billing and seats, and oversee everything. Sign-up makes you the Owner automatically.

HR

Run approvals, payroll, holidays, announcements and reports from the full admin console.

Manager

Approve your team's leave and reimbursements right from the app, with a dedicated approvals view.

Employee

Check in, track hours, view leave balances, apply for leave and file claims — all from the mobile app.
